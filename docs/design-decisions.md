# Design Decisions Log

This document tracks major design decisions, their rationale, and their charter compliance.

---

## How to Use This Document

When making a significant design decision:

1. **Document the decision** with date and context
2. **State the alternatives considered**
3. **Explain the rationale**
4. **Link to charter principles** that support it
5. **Note any concerns** for future review
