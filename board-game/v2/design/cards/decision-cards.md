## Card Writing Guidelines

### Good Card Characteristics

1. **No obvious "right answer"** - Every option should appeal to someone
2. **Coalition potential** - At least two ideologies benefit from each option
3. **Real tradeoffs** - Budget vs. Stability, short-term vs. long-term
4. **Historical grounding** - Based on real political dilemmas
5. **Clear language** - Options understood in 5 seconds
