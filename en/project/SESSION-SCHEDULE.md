# Design Session Schedule

Complete session plan from current state to first playtest and beyond.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Sessions 0-4)

### Session 0: Vision Alignment
**Duration:** 45 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Open | 5 min | Read `quick-start-v1.md` aloud |
| Share | 10 min | Each person: "What excites you?" |
| Share | 10 min | Each person: "What concerns you?" |
| Discuss | 15 min | Align on core experience goal |
| Close | 5 min | Confirm schedule, assign pre-reading |

**Pre-reading:** `quick-start-v1.md`
**Output:** Shared vision statement (1 sentence)

---

### Session 1: Player Identity & Factions
**Duration:** 60 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Context | 10 min | Read "What Players Represent" + "Factions" sections |
| Discuss | 15 min | Are faction leaders the right identity? |
| Discuss | 15 min | Are named factions (Progressives, etc.) problematic? |
| Decide | 15 min | Confirm or change player identity concept |
| Close | 5 min | Document decisions, assign next pre-reading |

**Pre-reading:** `core-rules-v1.md` sections on players and factions
**Decisions needed:**
- [ ] Player identity confirmed or changed
- [ ] Faction naming approach (named vs. abstract colors)
- [ ] Asymmetric bonuses: keep, modify, or remove?

---

### Session 2: Core Loop & Turn Structure
**Duration:** 60 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Context | 10 min | Walk through 6-phase turn structure |
| Discuss | 15 min | Are 6 phases too many? What could merge? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Deliberation: timed (5 min) or open? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Policy limits: 1 sponsor + max 3 pass per round? |
| Decide | 10 min | Finalize turn structure |
| Close | 5 min | Document decisions |

**Pre-reading:** `core-rules-v1.md` "Turn Structure" section
**Decisions needed:**
- [ ] Number of phases (keep 6 or simplify)
- [ ] Deliberation timing
- [ ] Policy action limits per round

---

### Session 3: Institutions Deep Dive
**Duration:** 60 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Context | 10 min | Review all 6 institutions and their mechanics |
| Discuss | 10 min | Are 6 too many? Which could merge/cut? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Judiciary: dice roll vs. deterministic review |
| Discuss | 10 min | Markets: separate mechanic or just Growth effects? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Public Opinion vs. Legitimacy distinction |
| Decide | 10 min | Finalize institution list and mechanics |

**Pre-reading:** `core-rules-v1.md` "Institutions" section
**Decisions needed:**
- [ ] Final institution count
- [ ] Judiciary review mechanic
- [ ] Markets mechanic
- [ ] Public Opinion handling

---

### Session 4: Victory, Loss & Tension
**Duration:** 60 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Context | 10 min | Review victory/loss conditions, feedback loops |
| Discuss | 15 min | Semi-cooperative: right tension level? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Collapse thresholds: too harsh or lenient? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Legacy priorities: interesting choices? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Governing Coalition: adds fun or complexity? |
| Decide | 5 min | Finalize victory structure |

**Pre-reading:** `core-rules-v1.md` "Victory Conditions" + "Feedback Loops"
**Decisions needed:**
- [ ] Confirm semi-cooperative mode
- [ ] Collapse threshold values
- [ ] Legacy priority list
- [ ] Coalition mechanic: keep or cut

---

## Phase 2: Content (Sessions 5-6)

### Session 5: Policy Card Review
**Duration:** 60 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Context | 10 min | Read 5-6 sample policies aloud |
| Discuss | 15 min | Do cards feel meaningfully different? |
| Discuss | 15 min | Are tradeoffs clear? Any feel unfair? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Ideological balance: any systematic bias? |
| Decide | 10 min | Cards to keep, cut, or revise |

**Pre-reading:** `design/content/policies/sample-policies-v1.md`
**Decisions needed:**
- [ ] Policy card template confirmed
- [ ] Problem cards identified
- [ ] Balance adjustments noted

---

### Session 6: Event Card Review
**Duration:** 45 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Context | 10 min | Read 4-5 sample events aloud |
| Discuss | 10 min | Are response choices interesting? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Severity mix: right distribution? |
| Discuss | 10 min | Any events feel arbitrary or unfair? |
| Decide | 5 min | Events to keep, cut, or revise |

**Pre-reading:** `design/content/events/sample-events-v1.md`
**Decisions needed:**
- [ ] Event card template confirmed
- [ ] Problem events identified
- [ ] Severity distribution confirmed

---

## Phase 3: Prototype (Sessions 7-8)

### Session 7: Playtest Planning
**Duration:** 45 min | **Participants:** All collaborators

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Review | 10 min | Summarize all decisions from Sessions 1-6 |
| Plan | 10 min | What's the minimum prototype? (paper, digital?) |
| Plan | 10 min | Who plays? When? Where? |
| Plan | 10 min | What are we testing? (pacing, fun, clarity?) |
| Assign | 5 min | Who creates what components by when? |

**Pre-reading:** `base-scenario-v1.md`, updated `CHECKLIST.md`
**Outputs:**
- [ ] Prototype component list
- [ ] Playtest date scheduled
- [ ] Testing goals defined
- [ ] Component assignments

---

### Session 8: Prototype Assembly (Work Session)
**Duration:** 90 min | **Participants:** Whoever is building

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Setup | 10 min | Gather materials, assign tasks |
| Build | 60 min | Create cards, boards, tracking sheets |
| Test | 15 min | Quick rules walkthrough with components |
| Prep | 5 min | Pack prototype, confirm playtest logistics |

**Materials needed:**
- [ ] Card stock or index cards
- [ ] Markers/pens
- [ ] Tokens (coins, cubes, etc.)
- [ ] Printed rules summary
- [ ] Tracking sheets

---

## Phase 4: Playtest Cycle (Sessions 9-12)

### Session 9: First Playtest
**Duration:** 120-150 min | **Participants:** 3-5 players

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Teach | 15 min | Explain rules using quick-start |
| Play | 90-120 min | Full game (or as far as time allows) |
| Debrief | 15 min | Quick reactions while fresh |

**Capture during play:**
- Moments of confusion
- Moments of excitement
- Rules questions that arise
- Pacing issues

---

### Session 10: Playtest Debrief
**Duration:** 45 min | **Participants:** All playtesters + designers

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Share | 15 min | Each player: favorite moment, worst moment |
| Analyze | 15 min | What mechanics worked? Which didn't? |
| Prioritize | 10 min | Top 3 things to fix before next test |
| Plan | 5 min | Schedule next playtest |

**Pre-reading:** Any notes from playtest
**Outputs:**
- [ ] Prioritized fix list
- [ ] Next playtest date
- [ ] Assigned fixes

---

### Session 11: Iteration Work Session
**Duration:** 60 min | **Participants:** Designers

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Review | 10 min | Confirm fix priorities |
| Work | 40 min | Update rules, cards, components |
| Verify | 10 min | Walk through changes, update docs |

**Outputs:**
- [ ] Updated rules files
- [ ] Revised cards (if any)
- [ ] Ready for next playtest

---

### Session 12: Second Playtest + Debrief
**Duration:** 150 min | **Participants:** 3-5 players (ideally some new)

| Segment | Time | Activity |
|---------|------|----------|
| Teach | 15 min | Updated rules |
| Play | 100 min | Full game |
| Debrief | 20 min | Compare to first playtest |
| Decide | 15 min | Major pivots needed or continue iterating? |

## Recommended Pacing

| Pace | Session Frequency | Time to First Playtest |
|------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Intensive** | 2-3 sessions/week | 2-3 weeks |
| **Steady** | 1 session/week | 6-8 weeks |
