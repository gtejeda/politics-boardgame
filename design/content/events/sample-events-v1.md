# Sample Event Cards v1.0

> 15 events demonstrating the range of severity, categories, and choice-based responses.
> Events are things that happen TO players, requiring response - not player-initiated.

---

## Minor Events (Low Stakes, Routine Challenges)

### EV01: Budget Shortfall
**Category:** Economic | **Severity:** Minor | **Timing:** Immediate

The treasury reports lower-than-expected revenue this quarter.

**Effect:** Budget -1

**Response Options:**
- A) **Accounting adjustment:** Legitimacy -1 (creative math)
- B) **Announce honestly:** Budget -1 instead (total -2)
- C) **Ignore:** Effect stands as written

*No roll required. Players vote on response.*

---

### EV02: Local Protest
**Category:** Social | **Severity:** Minor | **Timing:** Immediate

A community group organizes demonstrations over local grievances.

**Effect:** None immediately, but choice matters

**Response Options:**
- A) **Engage and listen:** Legitimacy +1, next Social policy costs -1 Influence
- B) **Disperse peacefully:** Stability +1, Civil Society Strength -1
- C) **Ignore:** 50% chance (roll 1-3 on d6) protest grows to Moderate event next round

---

### EV03: Minor Trade Dispute
**Category:** International | **Severity:** Minor | **Timing:** Immediate

A trading partner raises tariffs on select exports.

**Effect:** Growth -1 this round only

**Response Options:**
- A) **Negotiate:** Spend 1 Budget to cancel effect
- B) **Retaliate:** Effect stands, but place "Trade War" marker (+1 severity on next International event)
- C) **Ignore:** Effect stands

## Moderate Events (Meaningful Impact, Clear Choices)

### EV04: Economic Slowdown
**Category:** Economic | **Severity:** Moderate | **Timing:** Immediate

Leading indicators suggest recession approaching.

**Effect:** Growth -2

**Response Options:**
- A) **Stimulus package:** Spend 3 Budget to reduce to Growth -1
- B) **Monetary easing:** Markets Strength -1, but Growth only -1
- C) **Austerity preparation:** Legitimacy -1, but Budget +1
- D) **Ignore:** Effect stands, but if Growth goes ≤0, trigger Economic Cycle bust

---

### EV05: Public Health Concern
**Category:** Social | **Severity:** Moderate | **Timing:** Pending +1

Reports emerge of a concerning disease outbreak.

**Effect (when triggers):** Stability -2, Budget -2 (healthcare costs)

**Response Options (choose now, effect applies later):**
- A) **Immediate response:** Spend 2 Budget now. When triggers: Stability -1 only, Legitimacy +1
- B) **Monitor situation:** When triggers: Roll d6. On 4+: Effect reduced to -1/-1. On 1-3: Full effect.
- C) **Downplay:** Legitimacy +1 now. When triggers: Full effect plus Legitimacy -2

---

### EV06: Corruption Scandal
**Category:** Political | **Severity:** Moderate | **Timing:** Immediate

Media exposes corruption in a government department.

**Effect:** Legitimacy -2, Sponsor of last passed policy loses 2 Influence

**Response Options:**
- A) **Full investigation:** Executive Strength -1, but Legitimacy only -1
- B) **Limited inquiry:** Effect stands as written
- C) **Cover-up attempt:** Roll d6. On 4+: No effect. On 1-3: Legitimacy -4, Liberty -1

---

### EV07: Labor Strike
**Category:** Economic | **Severity:** Moderate | **Timing:** Immediate

Major industry workers walk off the job.

**Effect:** Growth -1, Stability -1

**Response Options:**
- A) **Negotiate settlement:** Spend 2 Budget. Effect cancelled. Inequality -1.
- B) **Wait them out:** Effect stands. Place "Unrest" marker (next Social event +1 severity)
- C) **Break the strike:** Stability +1 instead. Liberty -1, Civil Society Strength -1

---

### EV08: Diplomatic Incident
**Category:** International | **Severity:** Moderate | **Timing:** Pending +2

An international incident strains relations with a major power.

**Effect (when triggers):** Choose one: Growth -2 (sanctions) OR Stability -2 (security threat)

**Response Options (choose now):**
- A) **Apologize:** Legitimacy -1 domestically. When triggers: Effect reduced by 1.
- B) **Stand firm:** Legitimacy +1 domestically. When triggers: Full effect.
- C) **Seek mediation:** Spend 1 Budget. When triggers: Roll d6. On 3+: No effect. On 1-2: Full effect.

## Major Events (Significant Consequences, Hard Choices)

### EV09: Financial Crisis
**Category:** Economic | **Severity:** Major | **Timing:** Immediate

A major financial institution faces collapse.

**Effect:** Growth -3, Budget -2, Markets Strength -1

**Response Options:**
- A) **Bailout:** Spend 5 Budget. Effect reduced to Growth -1, Markets Strength stable. Inequality +2, Legitimacy -2 (public anger).
- B) **Controlled failure:** Effect stands. Place "Contagion" marker (if Growth goes ≤0 this round, additional -2 Budget).
- C) **Nationalize:** Growth -2 instead, Markets Strength -2, Liberty -1. Budget +2 (seized assets).

---

### EV10: Mass Demonstrations
**Category:** Social | **Severity:** Major | **Timing:** Immediate

Large-scale protests erupt across multiple cities.

**Effect:** Stability -3, all policies this round require supermajority

**Response Options:**
- A) **Address grievances:** Spend 3 Budget. Stability -1 instead. Legitimacy +2. Must pass 1 Social policy this round or effect returns.
- B) **Maintain order:** Liberty -2. Stability -1 instead. Civil Society Strength -2.
- C) **Call for dialogue:** Stability -2 instead (partial). Next round: if any player breaks a deal, Stability -3.

---

### EV11: Natural Disaster
**Category:** Natural | **Severity:** Major | **Timing:** Immediate

A major natural disaster strikes a populated region.

**Effect:** Budget -4, Stability -2

**Response Options:**
- A) **Full mobilization:** Effect stands, but Legitimacy +2, Executive Strength +1
- B) **Adequate response:** Budget -3, Stability -1. Legitimacy unchanged.
- C) **Delayed response:** Budget -2, Stability -3, Legitimacy -3

*No option avoids spending. Only question is how much and what tradeoffs.*

---

### EV12: Security Threat
**Category:** Political | **Severity:** Major | **Timing:** Immediate

Intelligence reveals a credible threat to national security.

**Effect:** Stability -2 immediately. Pending: If no Security policy passed this round, Stability -2 more next round.

**Response Options:**
- A) **Emergency measures:** Liberty -2. Stability +1 instead of -2. Executive Strength +1.
- B) **Targeted response:** Spend 2 Budget. Effect stands but pending effect cancelled.
- C) **Transparent approach:** Legitimacy +1. Effect stands. If Security policy passed, Liberty -1.

## Crisis Events (Game-Changing, All-Hands)

### EV13: Constitutional Crisis
**Category:** Political | **Severity:** Crisis | **Timing:** Immediate

A fundamental dispute over governmental authority erupts.

**Effect:** Legislature Strength -2, Judiciary Strength -2, all normal voting suspended this round

**Response Options (requires unanimous player agreement OR highest-Influence player decides):**
- A) **Uphold institutions:** All players lose 2 Influence. Effect cancelled. Legitimacy +3.
- B) **Executive resolution:** Executive Strength +2, other institutions -1 each. Liberty -2. Executive's faction gains +3 Influence.
- C) **Popular referendum:** Effect stands. Each player secretly votes. Majority faction gains +3 Influence, minority factions each -1 Influence.

---

### EV14: War Declaration
**Category:** International | **Severity:** Crisis | **Timing:** Immediate

A foreign power declares war or major hostilities begin.

**Effect:** Stability -4, Budget -5, all other events this round cancelled (war takes precedence)

**Response Options:**
- A) **Full mobilization:** Budget -3 more (total -8). Stability -2 instead. Growth -2. Game gains +2 rounds but final round is "War's End" special event.
- B) **Defensive posture:** Effect stands. Each round until resolved: Budget -2, Growth -1. Resolved when any Security policy passes OR 3 rounds pass.
- C) **Seek peace:** Legitimacy -3, Stability -3 instead. Immediate resolution but "Humiliation" marker (Nationalist faction gains +3 Influence, all International events +1 severity rest of game).

---

### EV15: Pandemic
**Category:** Natural | **Severity:** Crisis | **Timing:** Pending +1, then ongoing

A major pandemic threatens public health.

**First Effect (Round +1):** Budget -3, Stability -2, Growth -2

**Ongoing (each round until resolved):** Budget -2, Growth -1

**Resolution:** Passes when total of 8 Budget spent on responses across all rounds, OR 4 rounds pass (natural immunity).

**Response Options (choose each round):**
- A) **Lockdown:** Spend 2 Budget toward resolution. Liberty -1. Growth -1 extra.
- B) **Moderate measures:** Spend 1 Budget toward resolution. Effects as written.
- C) **Minimal response:** No Budget cost. Stability -1 extra. Does not count toward resolution.

## Design Principles Demonstrated

1. **Choices, not just effects** - Every event has response options
2. **Tradeoffs in responses** - No perfect answers
3. **Explainable causation** - Events have clear triggers and consequences
4. **Timing variation** - Some immediate, some pending
5. **Severity range** - From routine to existential
6. **Cross-resource effects** - Events touch multiple systems
